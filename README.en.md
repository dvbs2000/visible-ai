## What is this

Most AI explainers are either papers written for engineers, or clickbait screaming "revolutionary" and "mind-blowing." **This book is for ordinary people who want to *truly* understand what AI is really doing** — no math background, no coding required. You follow two characters, **Leo × Mia**, up a mountain, from "a single neuron" all the way to "writing the code for an AI app with your own hands."

> By the end you go from **"every AI term is noise"** to **"every term lands on a square of the map."**
> New model drops, new buzzword trends — you're no longer anxious. You know whether what changed is the architecture, the training, or just application-layer packaging.

## ✨ Why it's different

<img src="images/preview-illustrations.jpg" alt="100+ original illustrations and diagrams" width="100%">

- 🎨 **You can see it**: illustrated throughout — **100+ original drawings + vector flowcharts** — making "attention," "diffusion," "gradient descent" something you can actually *look at*.
- 🧸 **Not dry**: two characters, **Leo × Mia**, teach by Q&A; every principle opens with a "snap-your-fingers" little story.
- 🎯 **You actually learn**: each chapter is a **five-beat loop** — open with "the puzzle this chapter cracks," close with a one-sentence method "into your toolbox" + a self-test. You leave with something, not just a show.
- 🔍 **Demystified**: every AI term is **reduced to its real mechanism** — a neuron ≠ a brain, attention ≠ consciousness, an Agent's "persistence" is a hard-coded while-loop. **Anti-mysticism, see the essence.**
- 🚀 **Zero to builder**: 6 stages, 30 chapters, from "three nesting dolls" to "calling APIs, running models locally, hand-writing a RAG pipeline."
